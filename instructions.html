Instructions

  1. This Product Photoshoot Brief Builder (as the name suggests) is configured for product photography shoots.
  2. Fill out one form per product shot being planned.
  3. Print the generated PDF or screenshot for quick access and sharing on digital devices.
  4. PDF generator works on mobile and desktop Chrome and Safari. Performance on other browsers has not been tested.
  5. Depending on browser settings, pop up blockers may need to be disabled for it to function.
  6. None of the fields are required. You may generate a notecard with field titles but no field text to manually add any variables not found on the drop down menus.
