About

The Product Photography Creative Brief Builder offers a simple solution for organizing workflow during professional photoshoots. Use the dropdown menus to specify the technical, ambient and content delivery specifications for each item that needs to be shot to generate a printable note card with all the information needed to capture the desired image. Generate one such note card per item being photographed.


Fields on the form include; intended usage of content, format of files required, image orientation, camera lens to be used, photo lighting source, photo lighting add-on, camera angle, and camera perspectives. More elaborate art direction options will be added in future versions of this product.
